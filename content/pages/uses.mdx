- My desk is a [Steelcase Airtouch](https://store.steelcase.com/ccrz__ProductDetails?viewState=DetailView&cartID=&portalUser=&store=&cclcl=en_US&sku=XF5WX3WX8NU7US3RU8QM). It's a great desk, but I bought it before there were a lot more cheaper options, so don't generally recommend due to the price.
- My chair is an [Ergohuman ME7ERG](http://ergohuman.com/ergohuman-chair-me7erg-high-back-with-headrest-and-mesh/). I have a _really_ long torso, so most arm rests aren't tall enough for meâ€”this was the only one I found that worked for me.

- My keyboard is [Microsoft Sculpt Comfort](https://smile.amazon.com/Microsoft-Sculpt-Comfort-Desktop-L3V-00001/dp/B00CYX53QW/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1540267498&sr=8-1&keywords=microsoft+sculpt+comfort+keyboard+1531). - My trackpad is [Apple's Magic Trackpad](https://smile.amazon.com/Apple-MC380Z-A-Wireless-Trackpad/dp/B003XLYAWC/ref=sr_1_4?ie=UTF8&qid=1540267643&sr=8-4&keywords=apple+trackpad+1). Gestures ftw. BetterTouchTool makes this trackpad actually feel magical.
